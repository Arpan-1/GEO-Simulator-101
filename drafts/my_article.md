# The Ultimate Backpacking Gear Guide

## What is Backpacking?
Backpacking is the sport of hiking and camping overnight in the wilderness
while carrying all your gear in a backpack.

## Essential Gear You Need
Every backpacker needs these core items before hitting the trail.

### Shelter
A lightweight tent or tarp is essential for protection from the elements.
Look for tents under 1kg for ultralight backpacking.

### Sleep System
A sleeping bag rated for the lowest expected temperature and a sleeping
pad for insulation from the ground.

### Navigation
Always carry a paper map and compass as backup to your GPS device.

### Water Treatment
A water filter or purification tablets to make trail water safe to drink.

### Food and Cooking
A lightweight stove, fuel canister, and high-calorie trail food like
nuts, jerky, and freeze-dried meals.
